Rules

The rules are very simple: You need to guess the hidden five letter word in 6 tries.
To get started, just type any five letter word on the first line and press Enter.
If the letter is guessed correctly and is in the correct place, it will be highlighted in green, if the letter is in the word, but in the wrong place - it will be in yellow, and if the letter is not in the word, it will be dark gray.
